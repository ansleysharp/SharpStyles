SERVICES WE OFFER

Unibody Repairs

Our unibody repairs are very accurate beause we use a state of the art computerized, laser measuring system called Car-O-Liner. The Car-O-Liner system relies on ultrasound technology for dramatic productivity improvements and is the best in the industry for speed and accuracy. This system enables us to repair your vehicle using factory blueprint specifications.

Auto Painting

Together with Standox, David White Body Shop provides the highest quality refinishing and color matching available. We utilize the most up to date, sophisticated paint booths (Downdraft and Baking System) available. We offer a lifetime written warranty o all of our paint and body work.

Paintless Dent Repair

Have a small dent or ding? We offer PDR (Paintless Dent Repair). Many dents can be taken out without a trace or the original paint ever being disturbed. This can save you time and money.

Auto Glass

Windshield chips or cracks? We offer expert replacement. Our certified technicians can also quickly replace your side glass, vent glass, or rear windshield to get you back on the road fast.
